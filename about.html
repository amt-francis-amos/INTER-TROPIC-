We provide ground handling services which are not related to the actual flying of the aircraft but instead involves other tasks. There is more paper work and legislation involved than one may imagine. So when the airline is selecting a ground handler, they prefer one who has everything covered. In Aviation, aircraft ground handling defines the loading, unloading and or servicing of an aircraft while it is parked at a terminal gate of an airport.
