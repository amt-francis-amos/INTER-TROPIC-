OUR WIL'S GROUP INC. HISTORY

Wil's Bar, a division of Wil's Group Inc., is a vibrant entertainment destination offering a wide selection of beverages, delicious food, and a lively atmosphere. With two branches in Liberia – one located on the Robertfield Highway and the other on the Boulevard Road – Wil's Bar caters to patrons seeking relaxation and socialization. Established in 2021, Wil's Bar has quickly become a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike, with its commitment to quality service, an extensive drink menu, and mouthwatering food options. Whether you're craving savory appetizers, hearty mains, or delectable desserts, Wil's Bar has something to satisfy every palate. In addition to its flagship locations, Wil's Bar recently expanded its reach with a successful branch in Ghana, further solidifying its position as a premier entertainment and dining venue in the region.

WIL'S QUEST HOUSE

Wil's Guest House, another subdivision of Wil's Group Inc., provides comfortable accommodation for travelers seeking a home away from home experience. Our guest house offers a range of amenities and personalized service to ensure a memorable stay for every guest. Whether you're visiting for business or pleasure, Wil's Guest House is your ideal choice for accommodation in Liberia. Established in 2023 as part of Wil's Group Inc.'s expansion efforts, Wil's Guest House has quickly gained a reputation for excellence in hospitality, with plans for further growth and development in the future.

WIL'S CEMENT STORE

QUALITY AND AFFORDABLE

Wil's Cement Store is the construction arm of Wil's Group Inc., specializing in the distribution of high-quality cement products for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Established in 2021, Wil's Cement Store has quickly emerged as a trusted supplier in the construction industry, known for its reliability, efficiency, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you're a contractor, developer, or homeowner, Wil's Cement Store is your one-stop shop for all your cement needs. At Wil's Group Inc., we are committed to driving growth, innovation, and excellence across all our divisions. With a strong foundation and a clear vision for the future, we are poised to continue making a positive impact in Liberia and beyond.
