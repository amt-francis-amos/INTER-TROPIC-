OUR AIRLINE HISTORY

INTERTROPIC HOLDING & AIR SERVICES is an all-inclusive aviation service provider. we promote aviation safety and corporate integrity. We provide ground handling services which are not related to the actual flying of the aircraft but instead involves other tasks. There is more paper work and legislation involved than one may imagine. So when the airline is selecting a ground handler, they prefer one who has everything covered. In Aviation, aircraft ground handling defines the loading, unloading and or servicing of an aircraft while it is parked at a terminal gate of an airport. We are an accredited institution registered and licensed by the civil aviation authority (CAA). Also,vision to be the leading service providers interms of quality and value for money in the airports we operate. INTER TROPIC HOLDING provides flight support services, handling, aircraft charter solutions, cargo operations, Ticketing and hotel reservation. An airline chooses us then works directly with us as ground handling service providers to determine their needs. With this agreement, the airport authority doesn't require a separate operating agreement with the airline, and all billings can go through us. Below are the major parties involved in Ground Handling Services in our airport of presence. AIRPORT: This are the systems and equipment providers GROUND HANDLERS: This are the services providers AIRLINES: Customers who define services and standards to be received and paid for.

We're in the Customer Service business we just happen to provide airline transportation.
